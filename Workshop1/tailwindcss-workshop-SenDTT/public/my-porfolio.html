About me

My name is Thi Thanh Sen Doan, I was born in 1997 and am from Vietnam. I am so grateful because I live in an environment surrounded by love, support, and encouragement from my parents and siblings. They taught me the values of hard work, patience, and the importance of education. These values have guided me through various stages of my life, shaping who I am today. I love and miss my family so much.

In high school, I was excited about the subject of information technology when we studied the Pascal language. My love for this subject led me to pursue a degree in computer science, where I gained a solid foundation in programming, algorithms, and software development. Over the years, I have worked as a full-stack PHP web developer, honing my skills in both front-end and back-end development. This role has provided me with invaluable experience in building and managing web applications, collaborating with teams, and solving complex problems.

One of the big decisions of my life was to relocate to the United States and pursue a Master's degree in Computer Science at Maharishi International University (MIU). The graduate program at MIU is a stepping stone for me to achieve my personal and professional goals. I will upgrade my skills to expand my knowledge in the field and reach new levels in my career in the future. My personal goals for the graduate program extend beyond academics. Additionally, I aim to improve my English communication skills, particularly in listening and speaking, as this has been a challenge for me despite years of study.

As an introverted person, moving out of my comfort zone is one of the greatest personal challenges of my life and will change my mind. This will open a new chapter in my life. In conclusion, I am deeply grateful for the support of my family and the opportunities that have come my way. I feel both excited and nervous about the upcoming challenges in the new country, with many smart people from around the world and the knowledge at MIU.
